how to play

  • Invite a friend to play with you and draw who will be player 1 and player 2;
  • Player 1 starts the game by pressing the "roll dice" button;
  • If the dice value is greater than 1, the value is added to the partial value (next to the dice);
  • If the dice value is 1, the partial value returns to 0 and the turn passes to the opposing player;
  • Whenever the player wants to save the accumulated partial value, they must press the "hold score" button and this value is added to their total (next to the "roll dice" and "hold score" buttons);
  • The first player to reach 100 points in total wins the game.
